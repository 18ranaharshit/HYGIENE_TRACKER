## 🎯 About

**Hygiene Tracker** is a state-of-the-art sanitation management platform designed to transform how we monitor and maintain public facilities. By merging **Vision AI** with real-time location services, it provides an unprecedented level of transparency and efficiency in sanitation management. 

Whether you're a city planner managing a fleet of facilities or a user looking for the cleanest restroom nearby, Hygiene Tracker delivers a premium, data-centric experience that ensures hygiene is never a guessing game.

> [!NOTE]
> This project uses advanced AI Vision models to analyze photos of facilities, identifying everything from water leakage to surface cleanliness with near-human accuracy!

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andreasbm/readme/master/assets/lines/rainbow.png" />

## 🔄 How It Works

Experience the seamless flow of smart sanitation management:

1. 🖥️ **Command Center** — Start at the Dashboard for a high-level view of sanitation health across all facilities.
2. 🗺️ **Locate & Filter** — Use the interactive Map to find facilities near you, filtered by accessibility and availability.
3. 📸 **AI Audit** — Snap a photo of a facility. Our Vision AI analyzes it instantly to generate a hygiene score.
4. ⚠️ **Auto-Alerts** — If the AI detects issues, a maintenance ticket is automatically created and assigned.
5. 📊 **Optimize** — Track expenses and analyze trends to ensure resources are spent where they're needed most.

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 AI & Intelligence
- 🔥 **Vision AI Scorer** — Automated hygiene audits that grade facilities on a 1-100 scale based on visual input.
- 🔥 **Smart Issue Detection** — Instantly identifies water leaks, lack of supplies, or unclean surfaces.
- 🔥 **Actionable Recommendations** — AI provides a checklist of tasks to restore facility hygiene.

> [!TIP]
> The AI doesn't just score; it explains *why* a score was given and what specific cleaning supplies are needed to fix the detected issues!

### 🗺️ Location & Navigation
- 🔥 **Interactive Map Locator** — Full Google Maps integration with custom markers styled by hygiene level.
- 🔥 **Advanced Filtering** — Toggle between Paid/Free, Open/Closed, and Wheelchair-accessible facilities.
- 🔥 **Real-time Navigation** — Get instant directions to the cleanest facility with one click.

### 📊 Operations & Analytics
- 🔥 **Live KPI Dashboard** — Monitor average scores, total users, and active tickets in real-time.
- 🔥 **Expense Tracking** — Full financial ledger for sanitation costs, including supplies and wages.
- 🔥 **Maintenance Ticketing** — Professional-grade alert system for managing repairs and cleaning tasks.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Expense Tracker includes built-in trend analysis to help you see if your sanitation costs are increasing over time, allowing for proactive budgeting.

### 👥 User Experience
- 🔥 **Crowdsourced Ratings** — Star ratings and reviews from actual users to verify AI audits.
- 🔥 **PDF Report Export** — Download and share professional hygiene audit reports for any facility.
- 🔥 **Sleek Dark Mode** — A premium, glassmorphic UI designed for clarity and visual impact.
